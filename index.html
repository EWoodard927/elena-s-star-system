I'm Elena, an incoming senior at Signature School with a love for science and animals. This year, I'm trying my hand at computer science, a far cry from my normal work in biology and environmental science.


I plan on studying biology and environmental science in college before going on into a career in wildlife biology. I've already done some work in school, analyzing Indiana's dwindling eastern hellbender salamander population (pictured above).


On the side, I like to make my own costumes for Halloween and the annual GenCon in Indianapolis. Star Wars is a particular favorite of mine, as you can likely see by my themed portfolio.




At school, I'm the new vice president of our conservation club, where we raise money for local organizations, like Wesselman Woods, as well as global organizations, such as WaterAid. This marks my 4th year of active membership!
