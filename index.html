Hey, nice to meet you!

I'm Elena, an incoming senior at Signature School. I have a love for wildlife biology, but I'm also interested in the technological aspects of the field, such as satellite imagery.


I plan on studying biology and environmental science in college before pursuing a career in wildlife biology. I've already done research analyzing Indiana's dwindling hellbender salamander population (pictured above).


On the side, I like to make my own costumes for Halloween and the annual GenCon in Indianapolis. Star Wars is a favorite of mine, as you can likely see by my themed portfolio.




At school, I'm the new vice president of our conservation club, where we raise money for local organizations, like Wesselman Woods, as well as global organizations, such as WaterAid. This marks my 4th year of active membership!








Here are the projects I've been working on!



City Service Dog Initiative

The front page for a website that advocates for the rights of service dog owners. No relation to an actual organization. This project utilizes HTML and CSS.

Pokemon: Catapult Edition

A mesh between a choose-your-own-adventure game and a Pokemon battle simulator! Choose your Pokemon and defeat your rival. This project utilizes HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Lightsaber Color Quiz

Have you ever wondered what kind of lightsaber color you would have? Well, all you have to do is answer five questions to find out! This project utilizes HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

On top of being a scientist, I'm also a passionate violinist!


I've been playing violin since I was 5 years old, and it's such an important part of my life. While I play for my school orchestra during the week, I also participate in the Evansville Philharmonic Youth Orchestra. The most recent addition to my orchestral activities is the Evansville Youth Music Collective, a student-run organization in which we visit nursing homes to put on concerts, giving donations to fund Alzheimer's research.
